Local vs Global miss rates

local miss rate 
    how many of level 1 caches miss in level 2?

Global miss rate
    of all the requests made by processor, how many of those 
    ultimately miss in level 2?
    - tells more about the performance of the system

L2 local miss rate will always be greater than L2 global miss rate
